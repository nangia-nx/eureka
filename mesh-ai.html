How it works

From asking, to acting, to doing it without being asked.

Your teammates grow with you. Each one moves through four levels of autonomy — and you stay in control of how much you delegate.

01 — ASK

Ask anything

Plain English questions, instant answers. "How many of my people are on PTO next week?" Done.

02 — ACT

Get it done

Tell them what to do and they do it. Approve a stack of timecards. Process a transfer. Run a report. Send a notice.

03 — ANTICIPATE

One step ahead

They nudge you before you ask. "Payroll cutoff in 2 hours, here are the open items." "This shift is uncovered Saturday."

04 — AUTOMATE

Off your plate

When you trust the work, hand it over. Your teammates handle the repeat motions and only escalate the exceptions.

Why it works

Stop clicking through tools. Start working with a team.

Every other HR tool makes you click through 100 screens to get one thing done. Open payroll. Find the employee. Open their record. Click edit. Save. Go back. Repeat.

Mesh AI is different. A mesh of specialists does the work in chat, end-to-end — alongside your humans. They share one brain — one unified employee data model — so they know your people, your policies, your numbers. When Penny runs payroll, she's looking at the same source of truth as your check run. When Sage answers a paternity-leave question, it's your real policy, applied to that real employee.

One mesh. Every action. No dashboards required.
